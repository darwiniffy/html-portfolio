The Best Movies According to Darwin

My top 3 movies of all time.

It is difficult to make this list as there are a lot of movies that will make the list. Here are the three movies that I can think of.

Forrest Gump

I love this film as it is both funny and sad. Forrest will be taking you to a lot of adventures if you watch this film.

Arrival

The reason why this movie made the list as it is one of the most thought-provoking film I have ever seen. I love it for the unconventional storytelling where you are jumping through timelines.

Green Mile

This is the one where I cried the most. Green Mile is a very sad film about a falsely accused man. You should definitely watch it.
